Meet The Artist

DR. NEELSH KOTNIS

Managing Director, Kotnis Engineering

Dr. Neelesh Kotnis, the visionary behind Kotnis Engineering, is a highly accomplished professional with a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering, an MBA in Finance, and a PhD in Civil Engineering. With a profound understanding of infrastructure development, he has become a key figure in modern India's construction landscape.

Dr. Kotnis' expertise spans the entire construction process, from managing on-site operations to ensuring projects are completed within time and budget constraints. His strong leadership skills are complemented by his ability to effectively liaise with government agencies, clients, architects, consultants, and vendors. He oversees everything from determining technical specifications and land requirements to obtaining approvals and statutory clearances.

His passion for excellence and commitment to delivering high-quality results have been the driving force behind the growth and success of Kotnis Engineering, making it a trusted name in the interior design and construction industry.
